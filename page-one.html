Music Available In Manchester

Whatever you like Manchester has it, Rock, dance, urban, pop, classical and jazz. The music of Manchester is still flourishing, having talented artists and a diverse range of music. But Manchester is originally known for their indie rock and rave scene of 1980s Manchester. Bands such as the Stone Roses, the mock turtles and the charlatans etc had dominated the British indie music. Manchester arena is known to be one of the best venues in the UK and the second largest in the European Union it can take the capacity of 21,000 people, it is used a lot for hosting music events, so if you ever want to have a memorable moment with friends and enjoy a concert Manchester Arena is the place, as it was named “international venue of the year in 2022”. There have also been many iconic artists that have played in Manchester Arena such as Ariana Grande, Adele, Zara Larsson, and Anne Marie.(Pollstar,2023)

Manchester is a magical place, it has been described as a multicultural environment covered in diversity. There are so many skyscrapers and architecture to look and explore, walking around you find yourself lost in its rich and old heritage one of the most cultural and visited countries in the world. Another area to understand the beauty of Manchester is central library with its large collections of amazing books.
