The Manchester trams also known as the Manchester Metrolink has a that network consists of seven lines and there are 99 stops on the Manchester Metrolink network going as far as Bury and Rochdale, whilst running through the heart of Manchester. And as a student it is very cost friendly as you are able to buy a student rail card which reduces train fare costs by a third. It is also the easiest way to tour around Manchester and go sightseeing in Manchester’s most famous areas.

Manchester is considered to be a very bike friendly city as the city has dedicated many cycled lanes through the city centre and further. The transport for Greater Manchester even provides beginner lessons for those who have not ridden a bike in years. There are many reasons to go cycling in Manchester but one of the best reasons has to be looking at the beautiful nature Manchester has to provide, it also gives reassurance that you will reach to your destination on time. Overall riding bike has never felt so good.

Riding a bus benefits everyone, helps with being active and environmentally friendly, also reducing the amount of carbon dioxide emissions every day. Buses keep the air cleaner. The most popular bus in Manchester is the Stagecoach bus, it is always on time so there is no fear of being late, it is cost effective and cheap which is great for students and with just £300 a student can get an annual bus card that can be used as many times as they want. Learn more about travelling in Greater Manchester through The Transport for Greater Manchester website.
