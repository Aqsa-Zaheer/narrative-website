Art galleries and museums

Art Galleries And Museums

Manchester Museum

The Manchester Museum has a collection of 4,500,000 items from all around the world, an example of these numerous amounts of items would be fossils, world culture, and the ancient world, as well as archeology and natural history. Learn more.

Town Hall

Town Hall is yet another iconic piece of gothic architecture located in the heart of Manchester, where the clock tower reaches 85 meters high. "The Town Hall is a Grade 1 listed building, meaning that it is considered to be of exceptional architectural interest and national importance."(Manchester City Council, 2023). Learn more.

John Rylands Library

John Rylands library is known to be one of the most famous libraries in Manchester. The library itself is in the heart of Manchester and is available to all. It is also known for its exterior features are described as gothic architecture. Learn more.

Science and Industry Museum

Manchester is known for its contribution to science and technology, the museum of science and industry has numerous displays that celebrate this such as transport, power and computing. This museum has a variety of hands on activities such as games and science experiments. Learn more.

Royal Exchange Theatre

The Royal Exchange has one of the most beautiful architecture even after surviving WW2, it is a theatre as well as a shopping centre, the building is to be describe as modern and historic. Learn more.

Manchester Art Gallery

In Manchester art gallery you are able to find painting done by artists such as Gainsborough, Turner and Pissarro. Manchester art galleries mission is to become inclusive and open peoples mind to creativity. Learn more.
